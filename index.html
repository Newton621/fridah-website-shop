About Dressed By Fridah

Welcome to Dressed By Fridah, your premier destination for high-quality fashion items. Founded in 2020 by Fridah Kanini, our boutique has grown from a small local shop to a renowned fashion brand known for its unique style and exceptional customer service.

We carefully curate our collections to bring you the latest trends while maintaining timeless elegance. Our mission is to empower individuals to express their personal style through our carefully selected clothing, accessories, and footwear.
